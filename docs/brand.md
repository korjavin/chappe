# Chappe Brand Assets

## Naming

- Product: **Chappe**
- Mascot: **Chappie**
- Mascot role: **the little tower keeper**

Chappe is the CLI. Chappie is the little tower keeper watching Telegram signals.

## Visual direction

Chappie should feel open-source, observant, calm, useful, slightly whimsical, and never spammy.

Core motifs:

- green hood
- wooden Chappe/semaphore tower
- signal arm
- binoculars, notebook, lantern, map, or weather vane depending on feature
- soft cream background
- muted green palette
- simple outlines
- friendly but not childish

Avoid:

- robots
- sparkles
- paper planes
- generic chat bubbles
- growth charts as the main symbol
- Telegram logo imitation
